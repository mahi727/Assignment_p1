ABOUT ME

Hello, I am Afrina Sharmin, a passionate software engineer currently pursuing my Master’s degree in Software Engineering at George Mason University. With a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from the University of Dhaka, I have over three years of experience teaching as a Senior Instructor. I have also developed my abilities in web development, database management, and programming.

Afrina Sharmin



Throughout my professional journey, I have worked on diverse projects, from developing web applications for government initiatives to guiding students in their academic and professional growth. I’ve always enjoyed combining my technical expertise with a strong desire to contribute to impactful projects and solutions.
As the President of the Bangladeshi Graduate Students Association at GMU, I take pride in fostering a sense of community among graduate students. I focus on creating events and activities that help students connect, collaborate, and find resources for their academic and personal growth.
On a personal level, I am always eager to explore new technologies and challenges, as well as share my experiences to inspire and support others in their educational and career journeys. When I am not busy with work or studies, you will find me indulging in one of my favorite hobbies—baking. I love creating beautiful and delicious cakes, and I am thrilled to share my passion through my online cake shop.
